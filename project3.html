HI,I'm Abdelmadjid Tebboune

The current president of Algeria

Abdelmadjid Tebboune born 17 November 1945 is an Algerian politician
currently serving as the President of Algeria since December 2019
and as Minister of Defence. Tebboune took over the power from
former president Abdelaziz Bouteflika and former acting head of state
Abdelkader Bensalah. Previously, he was Prime Minister of Algeria
from May 2017 to August 2017. In addition, he was also Minister of Housing
from 2001 to 2002 for a year and again from 2012 to 2017 for five years.
